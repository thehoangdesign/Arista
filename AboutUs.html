Our Story

Made with love, forever remembered

We don't just believe in the gold standard, we live by it. What started out as designing jewelry turned into an industry rebellion. And let's be real, it was necessary.

Everything Has A Story To Tell

The inspiration for Arista's designs has always been the art of sculpting an accessory from the highest quality fabrics and embellishments. Years of experience culminates in the perfect headband shapes offering unparalleled comfort.

Our Design

We're a 21st century company so we use technology everywhere, especially when it comes to design. We're also direct-to-consumer so we always can keep a direct line of communication with you, our community-no middlemen messing around.

What we are chasing for

Mission

“Our mission is always to enhance customers' satisfaction, by diversifying products and services at highest quality and reasonable price, by advancing management and manufacturing system, and improving craftsmen's skills.”

Vission

“We aim to become the leading fashion jewelry brand in Vietnam with the core attributes being Creativity, Sophistication and Reliability.”

Core Values

Innovation & Creativity.
"Innovation is the foundation of the sustainable development of an organization - Creativity creates outstanding differences and competitive advantages."

Inspired by you

We are a new generation of fine jewelry!

Our inspiration comes from the drive to create a perfect balance between simple and bold, ordinary and odd, straightforward and luxury, and to push the limits where differences exist.
